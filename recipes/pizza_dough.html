Pizza dough

When it comes to making pizza, nothing comes close to fresh homemade pizza dough.

Ingredients

  • .35oz active dry yeast
  • 2 tbsp honey
  • 1 cup warm water (110째F, 44째C)
  • 350g(12oz) whole wheat flour
  • 4 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tbsp salt

Steps

  1. Dissolve the yeast in warm water and add 2 tablespoons of honey. Let it rest for 5 minutes or until it forms a creamy layer of foam. Then add 3 tablespoons of olive oil and stir.
  2. Put the flour in a large bowl and add a half tablespoon of salt.
  3. Pour half the water in the bowl, stirring until the dough pulls together and slowly add the remainder of water.
  4. Once the dough forms a nice solid mass, turn it out into a lightly floured surface and knead for about 10 minutes.
  5. Lightly oil the mixing bowl and place the dough back into it, turning it around the bowl to coat it. Cover with a cloth and let it rise in a warm place for at least 2 hours. (Optionally, you can put it in the fridge if you want to let it rise a little more for up to 48 hours, making it extra airy)
  6. Once the dough has at least doubled in size, you can punch down the dough and turn it out into a lightly floured surface. Divide it into the desired amount of portions and shape your pizza.
  7. Let the newly shaped pizza dough rest for 10 minutes before adding your toppings.
  8. Bake for 12 to 14 minutes at 220째C(425째F)
  9. Leftover dough can be frozen.
