Salmon Pizza

This delicious mix of smokend salmon, cream cheese and salty capres is a true delight for the senses.

Ingredients

  • 8.8oz (250g) of classic pizza dough
  • 3.5oz (90g) of cream cheese
  • 5oz (150g) of smoked salmon slices, torn into pieces
  • 1 tbsp of capres
  • 1 red onion, sliced
  • A handful of dill, chopped
  • Lemon wedges, to serve

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven at 428°F(220°C).
  2. Pour a bit of flour on your work surface and stretch the dough into a nice round shape.
  3. Put the dough on a baking tray.
  4. Spread the cream cheese on the dough using a spoon.
  5. Arrange the smoked salmon on the cheese and top with your onions, and capers.
  6. Put the pizza in the oven for 12 to 14 minutes.
  7. Once cooked, sprinkle the dill on top of your pizza and serve with lemons for squeezing over.
